Even strong business partnerships face disagreements over decisions, financial expectations, or future direction, and unresolved disputes can harm operations and threaten a venture's viability—especially when existing financial commitments like business loans or outstanding expenses are involved. OnDeck outlines six rules for owners: set clear expectations through a comprehensive partnership agreement, recognize warning signs, avoid premature conclusions, hold focused listening meetings, explore mediation and arbitration, and consult legal counsel for serious disputes.
